# Teriyaki Salmon Poke Bowl

> Yields  portions

## Ingredients

### Marinated Red Cabbage

- 1 kg Red cabbage — thinly sliced into strips
- 22 gr Fresh ginger — minced
- 10 gr Finger pepper (long) — sliced on the bias
- 1½ Whole Thai chili — sliced on the bias
- 960 ml Water
- 275 ml Rice vinegar (leaf / sheet)
- 85 gr Cane sugar
- 15 gr Salt
- 44 gr Sesame oil
- 25 gr Tamari SG

### Omelette

- 18 Eggs
- 50 ml Mirin
- 2 ml Salt
- 15 ml Black sesame seeds
- 5 Nori sheets — 2½ per baking sheet

### Brown Rice

- 1.6 kg Brown rice — short grain
- 3.35 kg Water
- 200 ml Rice vinegar
- 80 ml Cane sugar
- 20 ml Salt

### Vinaigrette: Japanese Wasabi and Seaweed Nori Dressing

- 340 ml Sesame oil
- 100 ml Grapeseed oil
- 175 ml Mayonnaise
- 150 ml Rice vinegar
- 90 ml Mirin
- 25 ml Rice sugar
- 1 Nori sheets
- 10 ml Wasabi powder
- 18 gr Green onions (scallions) — minced
- 3.75 ml Shichimi togarashi

### Edamame

- 600 gr Edamame — 1¼ bag

### Japanese Grain Mix

- 410 ml Pumpkin seeds — toasted
- 250 ml Black and white sesame seeds — toasted, each
- 160 ml Nori flakes

## Instructions

### Marinated Red Cabbage

- Remove from heat and add the cabbage, ginger, chili peppers, and oil.
- Let marinate and cool.

### Omelette

- Mix the eggs and add the mirin and salt. Mix well, then add the sesame seeds.
- On two small baking sheets lined with oiled parchment paper, pour a thin layer of the egg mixture.
- Sprinkle half the sesame seeds on top.
- Bake at 350°F for 5–6 minutes.
- Roll the omelette and wrap in cellophane with the unsealed sides tucked in; refrigerate.
- Lay ½ nori sheet along each omelette and roll tightly to enclose. Slice into 1 cm rounds and set aside.

### Brown Rice

- Rinse the rice well.
- In a 16 L pot, bring the water, rice, and salt to a boil. Once boiling, stir well, cover, and reduce heat to 2 for 45 minutes.
- Add the vinegar and sugar; mix well. Replace the lid and let rest 5 minutes.
- Refrigerate to cool.

### Vinaigrette: Japanese Wasabi and Seaweed Nori Dressing

- Blend with an immersion blender.

### Edamame

- Blanch in salted boiling water.
