# Goat Cheese and Cabbage

> Yields 30 portions

## Ingredients

**Vegetable Vinaigrette**
- 160 ml Olive oil
- 100 ml Cooking sunflower oil
- 80 ml Apple cider vinegar
- 70 ml Minced garlic
- 45 ml White wine
- 22.5 ml Honey
- 22.5 ml Fennel (ground)
- 22.5 ml Garam masala
- 10 ml Thyme
- 15 ml Salt
- 5 ml Pepper

**Vegetables**
- 4.2 kg Cauliflower — cut into small florets
- 2.7 kg Butternut squash — 1/2 L and 3/4 L pieces, 1/4 inch thick (net weight)
- 2.2 kg Green cabbage — wedges one inch thick (net weight)

**Almonds and Cranberries**
- 300 gr Slivered almonds — toasted at 350°F for 8 min
- 75 gr Cranberries — finely chopped
- 1 head Parsley — roughly chopped

**Finishing Vinaigrette**
- 280 ml Olive oil
- 85 ml Apple cider vinegar
- 22.5 ml Dijon mustard
- 30 ml Honey
- 7.5 ml Toasted fennel
- 7.5 ml Toasted cumin
- 10 ml Garam masala
- 2.5 ml Salt
- 1.25 ml Pepper

**Quinoa**
- 1.4 kg White quinoa
- 442 gr Red quinoa
- 102 gr Vegan butter
- 2.125 L Vegetable water
- 45 ml Vegetable chicken broth
- 7.5 ml Garam masala
- 20 ml Salt

## Instructions

1. (Vegetable Vinaigrette) Blend with an immersion blender.
2. Toss the vegetable vinaigrette with the vegetables in two mixing bowls.
3. Spread over three parchment-lined sheet pans. Roast in the oven at 425°F on high for 20 min, toss and return for 8–10 min. Let cool and transfer to a Tupperware. Yield: 5.450 kg
4. Mix the cranberries and almonds together.
5. Reserve the parsley for the garnish at the end. (Almonds and Cranberries)
6. (Quinoa) Rinse the quinoa thoroughly.
7. In a 16 L pot, add the quinoa, water, vegetable chicken broth, garam masala, and salt.
8. Bring to a boil. Once boiling, reduce heat to minimum and cook for 16 min.
9. Add the butter and mix well.
10. Let rest, covered, for 5 min.
11. Reserve for portioning. Yield: 3.940 kg
12. Feta: soak in brine water with 15 ml salt. Yield: 1.3 kg. Crumble for assembly.
13. (Finishing Vinaigrette) Same procedure as the vegetable vinaigrette!

## Plating

Portion per serving: Quinoa 125 gr (3/4 cup) · Vegetables 180 gr · Feta 45 gr (45 ml) · Almonds and cranberries 30 ml · Fresh parsley
