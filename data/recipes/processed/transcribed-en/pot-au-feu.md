# Pot-au-Feu

> Yields 39 portions

## Ingredients

**Broth : 18L Cauldron**

- 6 L Water
- 50 gr Shiitake mushrooms
- 35 ml Mushroom broth
- 175 ml Vegetable beef broth
- Sambal oelek
- 20 ml Thyme
- 10 ml Rosemary
- 7 leaves Bay leaves
- 7.5 ml Pepper

**Plant-based Beef (cut in 2)**

- 1.8 kg Plant-based beef (vege beef) — cut in 2
- 2.5 ml Salt
- 5 ml Pepper

**Vegetables**

- 80 ml Olive oil
- 500 gr Onion (1/4-inch pieces)
- 5 ml Dried hot pepper
- 1.5 kg Green cabbage — sliced 2 inches long
- 750 gr Rutabaga (1/2 pc)
- 500 gr Celery (1/2 inch)
- 800 gr Carrots (1/21 or 1/41 / 1/4 pc)
- 2 kg Potatoes 3/4 pc / 3/4 pc (red or russet)
- 1 kg Yam (1 pc / 1 pc)
- 750 gr Green beans — cut in 2

**Off-heat finish**

- 125 gr Miso AKA (red miso)
- 60 ml Bragg Liquid Aminos
- 3 heads (76g) Confit garlic
- 2.5 ml Pepper
- 63 gr/83 gr Potato starch/water

## Instructions

1. Heat the water and add all broth ingredients. When the mushrooms are tender, remove them, slice them thin, and set aside.
2. In a mixing bowl, season the plant-based beef with salt and pepper to taste. Roast in the oven at 375°F, low setting, on a baking sheet for 8–9 minutes.
3. Sauté the onions and cabbage with the hot pepper in olive oil and vegetable butter over medium heat (setting 4), 5–6 minutes. Add the rutabaga and cook 3 minutes. Add the celery, carrots, potatoes, and a pinch of salt to taste. Cook 4 minutes. Add the yams and green beans and stir well for 2 minutes.
4. Add the broth to the vegetables and bring to a boil, then simmer gently for 10–15 minutes. When the vegetables are tender, remove from heat and add the off-heat finish.
