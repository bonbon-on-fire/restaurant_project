# Vege Chicken Salad

> Yields  portions

## Ingredients

- 800 gr Vege Chicken — ground
- 290 gr Mango Apricot Chutney — reduced to a puree
- 1060 gr Ludovanne
- 70 gr White Balsamic Vinegar
- 70 gr Lemon Juice
- 32 gr Garlic — minced
- 40 gr Ginger — minced
- 10 ml Madras Curry
- 5 ml Turmeric
- 15 ml Scotch Bonnet Sauce
- 900 gr Carrots — small dice
- 1120 gr Celery — small dice
- 150 gr Shallots — sliced on the bias
- 120 gr Red Onion — thinly sliced
- 526 gr Cauliflower — small dice
- 60 gr Golden Raisins
- 60 gr Sliced Brown Almonds
- 1 head Fresh Parsley — chopped

**Mango Apricot Chutney**

- 10 gr Vege Butter
- 125 gr Onion — chopped
- 50 gr Orange Juice
- 175 gr White Wine
- 1 kg Mango — roughly chopped
- 137 gr Turkish Dried Apricot — roughly chopped
- 100 gr Sugar
- 5 ml Salt

## Instructions

Season the vege chicken with salt and pepper. Grill the chicken on a large baking sheet in the oven at 375°F on high, 9–10 min until golden.

Toast the almonds.

Cut the vegetables.

Mix all ingredients together.

**Mango Apricot Chutney** (in a 10-litre pot):

1. Sauté the onions in the butter.
2. Add the white wine and orange juice and bring to a semi-covered simmer.
3. Add the rest and let simmer uncovered for 20 min, until thickened.
4. Cool and store in 500 ml portions. Freeze the rest.

Yields: 5× grand recipe
