# SOUP: Ribollita

## Ingredients

### Broth
- 8 litres Water
- Cannellini liquid (bean juice)
- 170 g House vegetable bouillon powder
- 30 ml Thyme
- 15 ml Fresh rosemary — chopped
- 20 ml Salt
- 5 ml Black pepper

### Vegetables
- 150 ml Olive oil
- 750 g Onions — cut 1/3 lengthwise x 3/4"
- 5 ml Hot pepper
- 1 kg Carrots — small dice
- 600 g Celery — small dice
- 200 g Leeks — cut 1/4 lengthwise x 3/4"
- 25 g Garlic — chopped
- 1.7 kg Red potatoes — diced 1/2" x 1/2" (peeled)
- 1164 g Diced tomatoes

- Black kale (cavolo nero) — sliced into 1/4" ribbons
- 3 x 28 oz Cannellini beans — drained, reserve liquid for the broth
- 1 head Fresh parsley

## Instructions

1. In a 16 L stockpot, add all the broth ingredients and bring to a boil.
2. In a small saucepan, add 1 L of broth and 600 g of potatoes and bring to a boil for 9 min. Once the potatoes are cooked, purée with an immersion blender.
3. In a 21 L stockpot, sauté the onions and hot pepper in olive oil.
4. Add the carrots, celery, a little salt and sauté for 3-4 mins.
5. Add the leeks, garlic and potatoes; sauté for 2-3 min.
6. Add the tomatoes and bring to a simmer.
7. Add the broth and bring to a boil.
8. Check that the potatoes are al dente, then remove from the heat.
9. Off the heat, add: the kale, the beans, the parsley and the potato purée.
10. Taste - adjust.
