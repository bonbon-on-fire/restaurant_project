# Scallop and Shrimp Sauce

> Yields · 5.330 kg

## Ingredients

**To thaw**
- 1.5 kg Scallops
- 500 gr Small cooked shrimp

- 50 gr Oil
- 58 gr Vegan butter
- 500 gr Onions — finely chopped
- 2.5 ml Dried crushed hot pepper
- 300 gr Celery — finely chopped
- 10 ml Dill seeds
- 24 gr Garlic — chopped
- 60 ml Chicken bouillon powder
- 60 gr Tomato paste
- 65 gr Anchovies
- 30 ml Fish sauce
- 14 gr Lobster base
- 300 ml White wine
- 2 l 15% cooking cream
- 1 kg Creamy tofu (Horium)
- 10 ml Thyme
- 60 ml Parsley
- 3-4 ml Bay leaf
- 15 ml Dill umbel
- 100 gr Parmesan
- 30 ml Soy sauce
- 5 ml Pepper

## Instructions

1. Once the scallops are thawed, drain them and reserve the juice.
2. Cook the scallops over high heat without oil until they are opaque with a little colour, 3-4 min. Remove from heat and drain again.
3. In the same pan, over high heat, add the scallop juice and let it reduce until it becomes a golden caramel.
4. Deglaze with the wine. Add the lobster base and fish sauce. Mix well and set aside.
5. In a 10 l pot, sauté the onions and celery with the dill and hot pepper. 5 min.
6. Blend the garlic, bouillon powder, tomato paste, and anchovies into a purée. Add to the pot.
7. Blend the cream and tofu together with an immersion blender. Add the herbs and pour into the pot. Bring gently to a "blut-blut" simmer then let simmer for 30 min.
8. If the sauce has broken, pass it through a Chinese strainer and blend with an immersion blender until smooth and homogeneous.
9. Off the heat, add the scallops and shrimp, parmesan, soy sauce, salt and pepper, and mix well. Taste and adjust.
