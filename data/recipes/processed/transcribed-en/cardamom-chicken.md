# Cardamom Chicken

> Yields 33 portions

## Ingredients

- 1.5 kg Shiitake chicken texture — cut into 2–3 pieces according to size

**Broth (16L pot)**
- 2 L Water
- 44 gr Chicken powder (house-made)
- 24 gr Mushroom powder (Yamada)
- 7.5 ml Salt

- 431 gr Salted butter
- 1.2 kg Onions — 1/2 L or 1/4 L, 3/4 pc
- 1.6 kg Mushrooms — large slices
- 125 gr Fresh ginger, chopped
- 60 gr Garlic, chopped
- 45 ml Turmeric
- 15 ml Cardamom
- 8.75 ml Cayenne
- 15 ml Ground ginger
- 10 ml Cinnamon
- 2.2 kg Cauliflower florets — trimmed
- 890 gr Crushed tomatoes, Utopia (or Eden)
- 114 gr Tomato paste
- 500 ml Plain Greek yogurt 2%
- 500 ml Cream 15%
- 1 head Fresh cilantro, chopped

**Basmati Rice**
- 1550 gr Basmati rice
- 3 L Water
- 20 ml Salt
- 1.25 ml Whole cardamom
- 95 gr Salted butter

## Instructions

1. Place the chicken on a large baking sheet lined with parchment paper. Season the vege chicken with salt, pepper, and 5 ml of ground cardamom.
2. Bake in the oven at 375° on high for 9–10 min until well browned.
3. Mix and reheat the broth.
4. Sauté the onions in salted butter. 5–6 min.
5. Add the mushrooms. 5–6 min.
6. Add the garlic and spices. 2 min.
7. Add the tomatoes and tomato paste. 2 min.
8. Add the broth and simmer for 10 min.
9. Add the cauliflower. 5–6 min.
10. Add the yogurt, cream, and chicken and mix well. Simmer for another 7 min.
11. Off heat: add the chopped cilantro.
12. Taste and adjust seasoning.

**Rice**

1. Rinse the rice well. In a large pot over high heat, add the rice, water, cardamom, and salt. Uncovered, bring to a boil and stir well.
2. Cover and lower the heat to (1). Simmer for 10 min.
3. Stir once and let rest 5 min with the lid on.
4. Cool and set aside for service.
