# Competitive Landscape Scan

_Fast scan, June 2026. Positioning a web app for a small independent restaurant
(chef + baker), multilingual from day one._

## Summary

- Category is crowded but tilted toward **mid-market / multi-unit** operators.
  Serious tools run **$80–$430+/mo per location**, often gated behind sales calls.
- **Recipe costing + unit conversion + supplier ordering is commoditized** (table stakes).
- **Parsley** is the closest direct analog (recipe entry, supplier cost, weekly menu,
  nutrition sheets, order summaries) but US-centric, English/Spanish only, and stops
  short of paper-photo OCR, fixed-cost true-cost, and timing optimization.
- **Photo/AI recipe ingestion exists** at the high end (Apicbase ingests photos/
  handwriting/PDF) and in consumer apps — but bundled with affordable, multilingual,
  small-shop tooling it is still underserved.
- **Fixed-cost-aware "true cost"** (rent/power/repair into per-dish profit) — no tool
  found does this. Genuine gap.
- **Production *timing* optimization** (sequencing the oven across chef+baker) — largely
  unserved; tools plan *quantity*, not *when*. White space.
- **Season/weather-aware menu variety** exists only at the top (Apicbase) and needs POS data.
- **Menu *suggestion*** ("pick my week from my recipes + stock + season + variety rules")
  not found for a small shop without POS history. Open.

## Table stakes (don't lead with these)

Recipe entry/scaling/costing · unit conversion · weekly menu + printing · nutrition/
allergen sheets · supplier order summaries · production *quantity* planning · photo/AI
recipe ingestion (top tier + consumer) · multilingual UI (common in EU tools).

## Real gaps (lead with these)

1. **Fixed-cost-aware true cost** — overhead allocated into per-dish profit.
2. **Production *timing* optimization** — oven-sharing across chef + baker.
3. **Affordable, multilingual, photo-OCR onboarding** for a small shop.
4. **Stock/season/variety-aware weekly menu *suggestions*** without needing POS history.

## Caveats

- Could not confirm whether Parsley's "AI recipe upload" accepts photos of *paper*
  recipes (invoice-scan is a separate paid add-on). Verify before using as a selling point.
- Apicbase OCR robustness on messy handwriting unverified.
- "No tool does fixed-cost allocation" is absence-of-evidence, moderately confident.
